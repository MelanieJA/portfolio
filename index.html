About Me

Hi! My name is Mel and I am a web devleoper based in the South West of England. I design and build responsive websites, with a passion for creating accessible and intuitive user experiences.

Originally born in South Wales I moved to Bristol to study Psychology at university. During my studies I fell in love with the city and after graduating I decided to make Bristol my home. I’ve worked mainly with people with disabilities for the last 10 years which sparked my enthusiasm for creating beautiful web designs which are fully acccessible to any and all who use them. I’m always striving for considered designs that have a positive impact on peoples lives.

My Skills

I pride myself on building client centered, bespoke web designs that are elegant, accessible and user friendly. Whether you need a one page, or a multi page website my mobile first design approach ensures that every screen size is taken into account. With the added flair of animations to bring personality to your site your brand will ooze style and sophistication.
